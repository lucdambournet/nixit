# NixIt Creative System

## 11. Creative Briefs

### Brand Mood

- hopeful, modern, direct, human
- anti-corporate, anti-preachy, anti-clinical
- visual feeling: quiet confidence plus social energy

### Color Palette

- Soft cream: `#F6F3EB`
- Deep forest: `#17352B`
- Fresh green: `#6BCF77`
- Warm coral: `#FF7A59`
- Charcoal: `#1E1E1E`
- Mist blue accent: `#B7D8E8`

### Typography

- Headlines: Space Grotesk Bold
- Body: Instrument Sans Regular
- Accent or quote treatments: IBM Plex Mono

### Photography Style

- real dorms, apartments, sidewalks, cars, campus benches, messy desks
- close crops, handheld framing, natural eye line
- subjects looking thoughtful, relieved, honest, sometimes tired but not glamorized
- zero sterile studio aesthetic

### Lighting

- window light, hallway fluorescents, phone-screen glow, sunset gold, convenience-store spill light
- no overproduced beauty lighting

### Shot Composition

- vertical-first
- tight face-cam for honesty
- over-shoulder for app screens
- hands, desks, backpacks, water bottles, calendars for context

### Graphic Style

- minimal panels
- oversized plain-language headlines
- timer motifs
- clean rounded cards
- screenshots framed as proof, not decoration

### Motion Style

- fast first-second movement
- subtle zooms
- hard cuts
- caption-led pacing
- occasional freeze-frame text punch-ins

### Thumbnail Style

- large high-contrast text, 3 to 5 words
- one face or one clear app screen
- no cluttered multi-panel thumbnails

### Carousel Design

- slide 1: hard truth or question
- slides 2-4: explain or reframe
- slide 5: product proof
- slide 6: CTA

### Infographic Style

- plain-language labels
- simple arrows and timelines
- one insight per frame

## AI Prompt Pack

### GPT Prompt

"You are NixIt's senior lifecycle marketer. Write 5 short, plain-language onboarding messages for a new user who just joined a monthly quit cohort. Tone: supportive, direct, non-corporate, never preachy. Each message should be under 90 words and tied to a specific moment: signup complete, day before Nix Date, first craving, day 3 slump, day 7 encouragement. Include one CTA per message."

### Claude Prompt

"Act as a Gen Z behavior-change copy strategist for NixIt, a cohort-based nicotine quitting app. Generate 12 high-empathy ad hooks grouped by emotional job to be done: shame relief, accountability, starting fresh, friend-to-friend nudges. Keep every hook under 12 words. Avoid medical claims, fear tactics, and corporate phrasing."

### Gemini Prompt

"Create a 30-day cross-platform content calendar for NixIt using these constraints: 3 TikToks per week, 2 Instagram Reels per week, 2 carousels per week, daily Stories, weekly AMA, monthly cohort join push. For each asset include audience intent, hook, format, CTA, and metric to watch."
